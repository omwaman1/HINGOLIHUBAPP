Hingoli Hub - Account Deletion Request

Account Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your Hingoli Hub account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted:

  • Your profile information will be permanently removed
  • All your listings will be deleted
  • Your chat history will be erased
  • You will not be able to recover any data

How to Delete Your Account

Send an email to request account deletion:

  • Email: support@hellohingoli.com
  • Subject: "Delete My Account"
  • Include: Your registered phone number

Data Retention

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will:

  • Process your request within 7 business days
  • Send you a confirmation email once deletion is complete
  • Remove all personal data from our active systems
  • Retain minimal data required for legal compliance (if any)

Contact Us

For any questions regarding account deletion, please contact us at:
