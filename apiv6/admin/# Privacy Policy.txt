# Privacy Policy

Hingoli Hub - Your Local Marketplace

## 1. Introduction
Hingoli Hub ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Hingoli Hub mobile application and website (hellohingoli.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.
By using Hingoli Hub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect information you provide directly to us, including:
- Account Information: Name, phone number, email address, gender, and date of birth
                    when you create an account.
- Profile Information: Profile photo, address, and other details you choose to
                    provide.
- Listing Information: Details about services, products, businesses, or jobs you post
                    including descriptions, images, and pricing.
- Transaction Data: Payment information, order history, and delivery addresses for
                    purchases.
- Communication Data: Messages exchanged through our in-app chat feature.
- Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and
                    app version.
- Location Data: City and region information to provide localized services.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our marketplace services
- Process and complete transactions and send related information
- Verify your identity and prevent fraud
- Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages
- Respond to your comments, questions, and customer service requests
- Connect buyers with sellers in the Hingoli region
- Monitor and analyze usage trends to improve user experience
- Send promotional communications (with your consent)

## 4. Payment Information
Secure Payment Processing: All payment transactions are processed through Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. We do not store your complete credit/debit card details or UPI credentials on our servers. Razorpay handles all sensitive payment information securely.

## 5. Voice Calling Technology
In-App Voice Calls: We use ZEGO Cloud technology to enable secure in-app voice calling between buyers and sellers. This allows users to communicate directly without sharing personal phone numbers.
Call Data: Voice calls are encrypted end-to-end. Hingoli Hub does not record or store voice call content. ZEGO Cloud processes call data solely to facilitate the connection and ensure call quality.

## 6. Information Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information:
- With other users as necessary to facilitate transactions (e.g., seller contact information shared
                    with buyers)
- With service providers who assist in our operations (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
- With Razorpay for payment processing
- When required by law, legal process, or government request
- To protect rights, privacy, safety, or property of Hingoli Hub, users, or the public

## 7. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:
- Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS
- Secure authentication mechanisms including OTP verification
- Regular security assessments and updates
- Access controls to limit data access to authorized personnel

## 8. Data Retention
We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

## 9. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data stored with us
- Correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Withdraw consent for data processing

## 11. Children's Privacy
Hingoli Hub is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

## 12. App Permissions
The Hingoli Hub mobile app requests the following permissions:
- Internet & Network: To connect to our servers and display marketplace content.
- Notifications: To send you order updates, chat messages, incoming call alerts, and
                    promotional offers (optional).
- Location: To auto-fill business location when creating listings. Location is only
                    accessed when you tap "Use Current Location."
- Microphone: To enable voice calling between buyers and sellers within the app.
- Bluetooth: To support Bluetooth headsets during voice calls.
- Display Over Other Apps: To show incoming call notifications when the app is in
                    background.
- Vibration: To alert you of incoming calls and notifications.
You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

## 13. Data Deletion
Your Right to Delete Data: You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time.
How to Delete Your Data:
- Email: Send a request to support@hellohingoli.com with subject "Delete My
                        Account" and your registered phone number
- Web Form: Visit [hellohingoli.com/api/delete-account.html](https://hellohingoli.com/api/delete-account.html)

- Email: Send a request to support@hellohingoli.com with subject "Delete
                        My
                        Account"
- Response Time: Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days

[hellohingoli.com/api/delete-account.html](https://hellohingoli.com/api/delete-account.html)
What Gets Deleted: Your profile information, listings, messages, order history, and all associated data will be permanently removed from our systems.
Data Retention: Some data may be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or legitimate business purposes as required by law.

## 14. Data Safety (Google Play)
In compliance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements:
Data Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS. Sensitive data is encrypted at rest.
Data Deletion: Users can request account and data deletion as described above.
